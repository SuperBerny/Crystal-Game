Crystal Collector

You will be given a random number at the start of the game


There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.


You win the game by matching your total score to a random number. You lose if your total score goes above the random number.


The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.


Each time the game starts, the number to match will change and the value of each crystal will change as well.
